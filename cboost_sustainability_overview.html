Cboost strategic relevance
Cboost's elevator sensors extend equipment lifespan, directly reducing the need to manufacture new elevators and escalators — which consume significant raw materials. This aligns strongly with Germany's NCES goal to halve raw material consumption by 2045, making Cboost's proposition a natural fit for the German regulatory and industrial sustainability agenda. In the Netherlands, the combination of a strong circular economy baseline (30.6% circular material use, #1 in EU) and an established AI regulatory framework creates a receptive market for data-driven, sustainability-first solutions. Both countries reward companies that demonstrably reduce resource consumption — which is precisely what predictive maintenance technology does.
